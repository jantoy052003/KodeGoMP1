About the place

We are thrilled to welcome you to our vacation home, Serendipity Villa, so named as an homage to the serendipitous events that led us to this little piece of paradise.

If you want to stay in, you can refresh yourself in our 4ft deep, 3x5 meter dipping pool any time of the day. We have an outdoor shower and toilet accessible from the pool area. We even have a ‘poso’ or water pump. Try it; it works! Please make sure to shower and rinse off the sand before getting into the pool!

There is also a pergola perfect for outdoor dining or simply hanging out by the pool and enjoying the cool breeze.

Escape city life in our brand new, stylish & tranquil oceanside home with complete amenities.

The beach is just a 2-minute walk away, accessible via a safe, private, access road!

We are centrally located with the same famous waves as San Juan (only a 10 minute drive away from Urbiztondo!) but without the crowds. Perfect location to ride the waves, catch La Union's stunning sunsets, and throw your cares away!

Serendipity Villa is tastefully decorated with an open-plan design:

  • A cozy living area where you can Netflix and chill on our comfortable couch or rattan chairs;
  • A solihiya-accented kitchen, complete with everything you need to prepare flavorful meals. You may also opt to use the outdoor kitchen for some heavier cooking;
  • We have one bedroom at the ground level, ideal for three (3) guests, and two bedrooms upstairs, each with a double bed and suitable for two (2) guests in each room. All bedrooms have air-conditions. Towels are provided.
